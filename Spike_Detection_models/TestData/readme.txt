Dataset for test the single-channel and multi-channel spike detection model developed in Simulink

--------------------------------------------------------------------

monotrode_test_20 (single channel):
-Fs = 30kHz
-single neuron activity simulated
-30s recording
-colored noise level std 20µV
-no drift

monotrode_test_20_gt:
-ground truth of monotrode_test_20

monotrode_recording_20_waveforms_mean
-mean of the waveform extracted for the template matching



tetrode_test_20 (4 channel):
-Fs = 30kHz
-2 neuron activities simulated
-30s recording
-colored noise level std 20µV
-no drift

tetrode_test_20_gt:
-ground truth of tetrode_test_20

tetrode_recording_20_waveforms_mean
-mean of the waveform extracted for the template matching
